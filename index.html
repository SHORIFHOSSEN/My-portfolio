Movie Ranking Project

The best movie according to Shorif

Angry Bird

“Angry Birds” is truly an enjoyable animated movie for both kids and adults. With its vibrant visuals, funny characters, and simple storyline, it’s perfect for a family movie night.

Iron man

Although “Iron Man” is not a cartoon, it’s an excellent superhero film that appeals to both adults and older children. With action-packed scenes and a charismatic lead, it’s a fan favorite in the Marvel universe.

Mission imoissible

“Mission: Impossible” isn’t a cartoon either, but it’s an exciting action-thriller that’s best suited for adults and teens. Packed with suspense, stunts, and spy drama, it keeps you on the edge of your seat.
